Mother



A mother will make you clean your room


Even though nobody will be in it.


A mother can keep track of anything


Where I can hardly remember what I ate for lunch.


A mother will always have something for you to do


Even after a long day at work.


A mother will love you regardless of your mistakes


for this I am very lucky.


My mother is not my mom


She didn't give birth to me.


My mother is the best.


She loves me as her own.
